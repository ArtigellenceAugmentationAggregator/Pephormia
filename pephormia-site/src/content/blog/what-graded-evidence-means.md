Most of the internet sells peptides on the strongest-sounding claim it can find. We do the
opposite: we start from the peer-reviewed record and tag each finding by how strong it really is.

**Strong** — replicated human trials. **Human** — clinical but limited in scale. **Weak** —
small or mixed signal. **Mechanistic** — preclinical or in-vitro only.

The honesty line appears on every claim. That discipline is the whole product.
