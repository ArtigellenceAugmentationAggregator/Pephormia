The FDA's Pharmacy Compounding Advisory Committee is reviewing a set of peptides that
have circulated widely online. This is an education-only summary of what's on the agenda
and, just as importantly, what the evidence does and doesn't yet show for each.

We grade every claim honestly — strong, human, weak, or mechanistic — and we never present
mechanism as if it were proven human outcome. If the evidence is thin, we say so.

*This is educational content, not medical advice. Nothing here is a recommendation to use
any compounded or investigational product.*
